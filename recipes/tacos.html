Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 400g can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 200g cooked chicken, shredded (optional)
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • Fresh coriander, chopped (for garnish)
  • Lime wedges (for serving)
  • Sour cream (for serving)
  • Grated cheese (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the crushed garlic, ground cumin, smoked paprika, and dried oregano. Cook for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the chopped tomatoes and bring to a simmer. Cook for 10 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken slightly.
  4. Add the black beans and cooked chicken (if using) to the pan. Stir well to combine and cook for another 5 minutes until heated through. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Warm the corn tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until pliable.
  6. To assemble the tacos, spoon the filling onto each tortilla. Garnish with fresh coriander, a squeeze of lime juice, sour cream, and grated cheese as desired.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
