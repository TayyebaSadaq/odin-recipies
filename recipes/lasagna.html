Lasagna Recipe

Back to Home

This classic lasagna recipe is made with layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella cheese. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions!

Ingredients:

  • spinach
  • ricotta cheese
  • mozzarella cheese
  • parmesan cheese
  • ground beef
  • tomato sauce
  • lasagna noodles
  • onion
  • garlic
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • Italian seasoning

Instructions:

  1. place steamer insert into saucepan + fill with water to boil
  2. add spinach, cover and steam till bright green
  3. remove spinach, rinse with cold water + squeeze out excess water
  4. chop spinach + set aside
  5. in a large bowl, combine ricotta, 1 cup mozzarella, parmesan, spinach, egg, salt, pepper + mix well
  6. in a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat
  7. add onion + garlic, cook till softened
  8. add ground beef, cook till browned
  9. add tomato sauce + Italian seasoning, simmer for 15 minutes
  10. preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  11. in a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce
  12. layer with lasagna noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce + repeat layers
  13. top with remaining mozzarella cheese
  14. cover with foil + bake for 25 minutes
  15. remove foil + bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly + golden
  16. let lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving
